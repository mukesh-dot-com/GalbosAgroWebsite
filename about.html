About Galbos Agro

When Quality Meets Trust

Our Story

Galbos Agro was established as a professional agro company to streamline the supply of quality maize from regional traders to poultry feed manufacturers. Operating across five states with a network of 80+ verified traders, we have built a reputation for reliability, quality, and transparency.

With over 1.75 million kilograms procured till date, we have become a trusted intermediary connecting premium maize suppliers with leading feed factories across India.

Our Mission

To serve as a reliable and professional maize dealer connecting quality suppliers with feed manufacturers. We are committed to ensuring premium quality maize reaches the right factories at the right time, while maintaining transparency and fair practices in every transaction. Our goal is to be the trusted link in the poultry feed supply chain.
